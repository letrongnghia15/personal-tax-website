Who We Are

Tax work, done with care and precision.

A certified, credentialed tax professional committed to treating each return like the only one on the desk.

Sea Tax Services was built on a simple idea: tax preparation shouldn't feel like a commodity. Every filer deserves accuracy, attention, and someone who actually picks up the phone.

With over five years preparing returns for individuals, freelancers, and small business owners across multiple states, I've seen what happens when returns are rushed or automated past the point of oversight — missed deductions, flagged audits, and clients stuck holding the bag. My practice exists to prevent that.

Whether you're filing a first W-2, managing an LLC with employees, or untangling several years of unfiled returns, you'll work with me directly — from the initial consultation to the moment we e-file.
