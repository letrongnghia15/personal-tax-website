Google Form Coming Soon

Follow the steps below to embed your Google intake form here. Once set up, clients will fill it out directly on this page and responses go straight to your Gmail.

Setup Steps (5 minutes)

  1. Go to forms.google.com and sign in with your Google account (seataxservice@gmail.com).
  2. Click Blank Form and title it "Sea Tax Services — Client Intake".
  3. Add the questions listed below (or copy from the suggested list).
  4. Click Send (paper airplane icon, top right) → click the < > Embed tab → copy the iframe code.
  5. Open intake.html in a text editor, find this placeholder section, delete it, and paste the iframe code in its place.
  6. In Google Forms → Responses tab → click the envelope icon to enable email notifications to seataxservice@gmail.com.

Suggested Google Form Questions

  1. Full Name — Short answer (required)
  2. Email Address — Short answer (required)
  3. Phone Number — Short answer
  4. Tax Year Needed — Dropdown: 2024, 2023, 2022, Multiple Years
  5. Filing Status — Multiple choice: Single, Married Filing Jointly, Married Filing Separately, Head of Household
  6. Type of Return — Multiple choice: Individual, Small Business, Both
  7. Income Sources — Checkboxes: W-2, Self-Employment/1099, Rental Income, Investments, Social Security, Retirement/Pension, Unemployment, Other
  8. Deductions / Credits — Checkboxes: Homeowner, Charitable Donations, Student Loan Interest, Child/Dependent Care, Medical Expenses, Business Expenses, Energy Credits, None
  9. Major Life Changes This Year — Checkboxes: Married/Divorced, New Child, Bought/Sold Home, Started a Business, Changed Jobs, Moved States, None
  10. Business Type — Multiple choice (if applicable): Sole Proprietor, LLC, S-Corp, Partnership, Not Applicable
  11. Documents You Have Ready — Checkboxes: W-2(s), 1099(s), Last Year's Return, Business Records, Bank Statements, None Yet
  12. Preferred Contact Method — Multiple choice: Phone Call, Email, Text Message, Video Call
  13. Best Time to Reach You — Short answer (e.g., "Weekday afternoons")
  14. Additional Notes or Questions — Paragraph (optional)
